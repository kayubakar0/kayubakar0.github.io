Gadeva

Game world themed adventure game with a core game focus on mini games. The story of this game tells of a student who wants to become a game developer but suddenly gets stuck in the game world.

How to Play

  1. Open Gadeva Website
  2. Click run game an wait
  3. Play game

Story

In Gadeva player will accept challenges from the boss in the form of mini games, players will get an achievement if they win it. When a new achievement is obtained, a new challenge will open. When all achievements are obtained, a portal will appear that will bring players back to their original world.
