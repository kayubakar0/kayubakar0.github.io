Gamelan Metaverse Udinus

Gamelan Metaverse is a virtual reality (VR) application developed by Udinus University as part of a major campus project. The application allows users to learn and play gamelan, a traditional Indonesian musical instrument, in a virtual world. I was a part of the development team, which allowed me to choose this topic for my undergraduate thesis.

Tech Use

  1. Engine : Unity Engine 2020
  2. Plugin : XR Interaction Toolkit 2.0, Photon Voice, and Photon PUN 2.
  3. Device : Oculus Quest 2

Story

As a fresh graduate of the Agate Academy game development program, I was eager to put my skills to the test. I was thrilled when I was invited to join a large-scale VR gamelan metaverse project at Dian Nuswantoro University. I was assigned to the Unity programming team, and I worked alongside my friend to develop the first two stages of the project. The first stage was a simple VR game that could be played offline. The second stage was more challenging, as it required us to create a game that could play nine different gamelan instruments online and simultaneously. I had never used Photon before, so I had to learn a lot about it in a short amount of time. But with hard work and dedication, we were able to complete the second stage of the project. The project was a success, and it was a great learning experience for me. I learned a lot about game development, and I made some new friends along the way. I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked on such a challenging and rewarding project.
