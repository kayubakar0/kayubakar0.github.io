VR Mobility

VR Mobility is a VR app used for stroke rehabilitation walking exercises. The app uses VR technology to create a safe and realistic environment for stroke patients to practice walking. VR Mobility has the potential to improve the motivation, effectiveness, and accessibility of stroke rehabilitation walking exercises.

Tech Use

  1. Engine : Unity Engine 2020
  2. Plugin : Plugin Steam VR, Open XR, Kat Plugin
  3. Device : Oculus Quest 2 and KAT VR

Story

Developing a VR mobility app using Unity engine and Steam VR plugin integrated with KAT VR was a challenging experience. The KAT VR hardware was unstable, requiring frequent coordination with customer support in China. The tight 3-week deadline also added to the pressure. Despite these challenges, the team worked hard and overcame them to deliver a working prototype that met the client's requirements.
